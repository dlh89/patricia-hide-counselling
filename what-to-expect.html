What to Expect

I live and run my counselling practice in Eastbourne, East Sussex where I provide face to face, and online sessions via Zoom from a safe, and comfortable environment. I have experience off and work with a wide range of issues such as:

  • any form of loss, bereavement, relationship breakdown, loss of employment or the death of a beloved pet.
  • individuals who are in recovery from alcohol and substance misuse, relationship difficulties,
  • sexual abuse,
  • anxiety,
  • depression and low self-esteem.

In the first session we will spend some time talking about what has brought you to counselling, your current circumstances for instance: relationships, your history, and any support networks that you may have around you.

We will also discuss and agree to a therapeutic contract (this is not-legally binding) which includes my commitment to you and confidentiality, information on data protection, payment of fees, cancellation policy.
