About Patricia

Patricia is a member of, and registered with, the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) with experience supporting adults with a wide range of issues, including loss and bereavement, recovery from alcohol and substance issues, relationship difficulties, sexual abuse, anxiety, depression and low self-esteem.
